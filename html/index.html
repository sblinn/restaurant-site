Water Buffalo Bar & Grill has been a family-owned restaurant in rural Minnesota since 1956. Best known for our spectacular outdoor views and delicious dishes. Reserve window seating and enjoy the birds at the feeders and the local wildlife that roam freely across the nature reserves surrounding the property. Or come have a drink at our bar.
